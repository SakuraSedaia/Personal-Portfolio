Who is I?

I am an independant Year old YouTube content creator and fast food worker with too much freetime that has partnered with multiple studios. I got my start in 2012 with the creation of my first Channel, at the time... named PokeKid206, yeah terrible name right? Well that channel got me started inside the YouTube atmosphere, though about 2 years later it would be pretty well abandoned as I made the complete change over to my current Channel, originally called Ender GFX but has evolved into my current personality and name, Sakura Sedaia.


Sometime in October 2018 I started in a small Machinima "Studio" called Alpha Studios, where I would begin making a name for myself as a 3D Minecraft Animator and Render artist.
