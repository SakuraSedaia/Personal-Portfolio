Who am I?

I, Sakura Sedaia, am a Freelance 3D Artist who enjoys the occasional animation and render project, I make almost all aspects of my animations and renders myself. This is ranging from Rigs, Models, Character Designs, Lighting, and Compositing.

I primarily work with Minecraft Animation, as it is the area of 3D Art that I know the best, although I am yerning to learn more about different sectors of 3D Art in the future, I often have to work around a busy work schedule, as 3D Artistry is mainly a hobby for me, and not my actual Career.

My Secondary passtime is Website Development and Design, of which you see on this website. It is not a common hobby and one that I do work for others on, but it allows me to be independant when it comes to hosting, building, and working on my own websites such as this one.
