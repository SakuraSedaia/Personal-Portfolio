Who am I?

I, Sakura Sedaia, am an Engineering College Student who enjoys the occasional animation and render project, I make almost all aspects of my animations and renders myself. This is ranging from Rigs, Models, Character Designs, Lighting, and Compositing. I primarily work with Minecraft Animation, as it is the area of 3D Art that I know the best, although I am yerning to learn more about different sectors of 3D Art in the future, I often have to work around a busy work schedule, as 3D Artistry is mainly a hobby for me, and not my actual Career.

My Secondary passtime is Website Development and Design, of which you see on this website. It's a rare passtime for when I am bored of Animation and Video Games. I don't often provide website development work, though I do semi-actively develop and maintain https://frostlight-studios.org/, who is a small, startup Minecraft animation studio I work with and provide private builds of SACR.'
