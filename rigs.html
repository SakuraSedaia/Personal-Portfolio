? FAQ ?

Q: What Software do these Rigs require?
A: All Rigs posted on this page are made and tested in Blender 2.83.15 and newer LTS Builds, so any version of Blender newer than 2.83 can use these rigs seemelessly, but be cautious trying to use these rigs in anything older (Such as 2.79 or older) as the Drivers and Scripts (If any) can and will break.
Q: When will the Wiki become available?
A: The exact timeframe of when the Wiki for all my rigs is unknown, but I am putting work into the site as much as I can while maintaining all my other projects. The WIP Version of the wiki is online right now.
