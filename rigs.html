Sakura's Advanced Character Rig

DISCLAIMER: Rig is Discontinued and will no longer recieve updates part from patches to ensure compatibility with Blender 3.0 and newer

Notable Features

  • 125 Sharp Bends
  • Inverse Kinimatics for limbs w/ Wrists
  • Custom Armour Mesh w/ 4 different tiers
    • Leather Armour IS able to be dyed
  • Fully Functioning Facerig
  • Molars for your Demonic or Feline-like Characters
  • Advanced Eye Model & Material
  • Simple enough for Beginners, Advanced enough for experts
  • Female Body Mesh & Eyelashes
  • And Much, much more!

Compatible With:*

  • Blender 2.80
  • Blender 2.81
  • Blender 2.82
  • Blender 2.83 LTS
  • Blender 2.90
  • Blender 2.91
  • Blender 2.92
  • Blender 2.93 LTS (Recomended)
  • Blender 3.0**

Blender 3.0 may be compatible, however it is not officially supported by the Rig
