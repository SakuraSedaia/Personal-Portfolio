Q: What Software do these Rigs require?
A: All Rigs posted on this page are made and tested in Blender 2.83.15, so any version of Blender newer than 2.8 can use these rigs seemelessly, but be cautious trying to use these rigs in anything older (Such as 2.79 or older) as the Drivers and Scripts (If any) can and will break.
