The Head Region

The head region is easily one of the most complex parts of SACR, including both a simple to use Emotion system and a more advanced system for the more advanced animators and renderers to give a greater degree of control over the Facerig

Simple Emotion System

The Simple Emotion system is designed to be simple and easy to use, yet still be able to deliver good emotions without the need for pinpoint controls. There are 5 main controllers on the Simple face system:

  1. Eyebrow Master Controller
  2. Eyelid Master Controller
  3. Face Position/Eyeheight Controller
  4. Pupil Controller
  5. Mouth Master Controller
  6. Mouth State Controller

Eyebrow Master Controller

The eyebrow controller has 3 different controls attatched to the different forms of Transformation, the primary function is controlled by translation, and depending on how you move it will cause the Eyebrows to either slant inward or outwards.
